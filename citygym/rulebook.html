City Gym rulebook

  1. The membership fee is valid for 30 or 45 days (depending on the training package) from the date of the first training and is not transferable to another person.
  2. Please arrive a few minutes earlier than scheduled so that the training can start on time.
  3. Please respect the time of the scheduled training. If you are prevented from attending a scheduled workout, cancel the same in a timely manner within 12 hours so we would be able to cancel the coach on time. Training cancellations that arrive less than 12 hours prior to training will not be considered valid!
  4. You can take a break only in case of illness or an urgent trip, and register it in advance by phone or in person at the City Gym. The break can last for a maximum of one week in the current month and cannot be registered later.
  5. Do not come to training when you have a cold, because you can worsen your condition, but also damage the health of others.
  6. Use clean and suitable shoes and clothes for the gym! The dress code of the City Gym is prominently displayed.
  7. Do not use a mobile phone during training. Turn off your cell phone so as not to disturb other exercisers. If you are expecting an important call, please turn on the vibration.
  8. Don't carry valuables to training, in case of loss, the club is not responsible! Valuables can be left by members at the reception desk during training.
  9. For hygienic reasons, it is desirable to bring your own mat (towel).
  10. All dumbbells, plates, bars, benches, mats and other exercise aids have their place and members are obliged to put them back in place after use.
  11. When enrolling, members fill in the application form, and with their signature they accept the rules of the fitness club and exercise at their own risk.

Necessary equipment

  • Sports shoes (preferably running shoes).
  • Light clothing suitable for temperature and weather (not windbreaker and similar clothing which is airtight, or too many layers of clothing for the purpose of sweating).
  • Relaxed tracksuits in which you feel unrestrained, unopened.
  • T-shirts without a neckline, long enough to cover the lower part of the tracksuit.
  • You come to the gym exclusively for exercise.

What to pay attention to

  • Carry water with you and drink it throughout your workout.
  • Do not overexert yourself (for beginner exercisers).
  • Do not wear revealing clothes, clothing must be relaxed not tight.
  • Listen to your body (sometimes you will be able to do more, and sometimes less, depending on many elements, such as exercise time, weather situation, day of the month, stress in everyday life).
  • Find an exercise partner for motivation.
