What does it look like?

Although each trainee receives their own individual program, all depending on the goals of the training and the current state of the trainee, there are general items that the training contains:

  •  a wide range of stretching and shaping exercises
  •  elements of pilates, and ground exercises
  •  work strength on devices and with weights
  •  aerobics with elements of martial arts
  •  plyometrics
  •  cardio program
  •  breathing exercises
  •  a wide range of special stretching exercises
  •  nutrition program

Cardio boxing

Another treat for all our members.


Cardio Boxing is an explosive and dynamic cardio training intended for everyone who wants to lose weight in an interesting and effective way. This training combines punches from different martial arts (mostly from boxing and kickboxing), uses both hand and foot techniques on the bag and fits them into simple choreographic blocks.
